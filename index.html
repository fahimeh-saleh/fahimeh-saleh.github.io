I am a Postdoctoral Fellow in The Australian e-Health Research Centre (AEHRC) which is AEHRC is a joint venture between CSIRO and the Queensland Government, through Queensland Health. I am working on a personalised support platform using AI technologies to leverage large scale electronic health records for profiling cohort AMR exposures to enhance the precision of antibiotic treatment.

Prior to that, I was a PhD student at Monash University working on Natural Language Processing and Deep Learning under supervision of Professor Wray Buntine. I was also an NLP research engineering intern at Naver Labs Europe, France, working on contextual machine translation and text generation.

My research interests are in the fields of NLP and machine learning, with a focus on machine translation and text generation. More recently, I have been working on finding effective knowldge transition solutions for problems that are grounded in low-resource text generation scenarios.
