Fay Saleh

Unit 10, 71 Union Street, Brighton East 3187, Australia, fay.saleh@dolanto.com.au

Fay Saleh, Ph. D., is a highly accomplished senior AI scientist with a strong academic background and rich industry experience. She holds a doctoral degree in Generative AI from Monash University and has worked as a Postdoctoral Research Fellow at CSIRO in The Australian e-Health Research Centre (AEHRC), where her focus was on developing AI-driven personalized support platforms with the aim of automating cancer staging and detecting Antimicrobial Resistance (AMR).

Additionally, Fay gained valuable industry experience from her work at NAVER LABS Europe (formerly known as Xerox Research Centre Europe - XRCE) in France, where she specialized in generative models.

Moreover, Fay served as a senior AI scientist at Oracle, a renowned technology company at the forefront of innovation with its cloud-based platforms and enterprise software. In her role, she contributes to advancements in Digital Assistants through cutting-edge Deep Learning technology, shaping the next generation of Digital Assistants to provide intelligent, integrated, and secure solutions.

In her current capacity, Fay is at the helm of AI Projects within Dolanto, an innovative dynamic startup. Leveraging her extensive expertise as a senior AI scientist, Fay takes on a pivotal leadership role, guiding the company's AI initiatives to new heights.
