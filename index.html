Fahimeh Saleh

Room 405, Building 6, Faculty of IT, Monash University, Melbourne, VIC, Australia fahimeh.saleh@monash.edu

I am a PhD student at Monash University working on Natural Language Processing and Deep Learning under supervisions of Gholamerza Haffari and Wray Buntine. I was also an NLP research engineering intern at Naver Labs Europe, France, working on contextual machine translation and text generation.

My research interests are in the fields of NLP and machine learning, with a focus on document machine translation and text generation. More recently, I'm working on finding efficient and effective solutions for problems that are grounded in applications such as contextual NMT and data-to-document generation.
